About Jordan

Extensions and volume,
done flawlessly

Jordan Ellis has built her reputation on one simple principle: extensions should look and feel like they're part of your natural hair. She's certified in three primary extension methods — hand-tied, tape-in, and keratin bond — and brings personalized expertise to every client. Whether you're looking to add length, density, or completely transform your hair's volume and movement, Jordan's meticulous attention to placement and blending ensures results that are seamless, comfortable, and built to last.

Beyond her extension expertise, Jordan is an excellent precision cutter with a gift for creating shapes that move naturally. She understands how volume, length, and technique work together to create flattering silhouettes. Her approach to extensions isn't just about adding hair — it's about complementing your cut, understanding your lifestyle, and creating something you'll feel confident wearing every day.

Jordan's clients know her for her calm, collaborative approach and her commitment to hair health. She educates every client on proper care and maintenance, extending the life of their extensions while minimizing damage. At Craft Collective, she represents the team's dedication to both artistry and client wellbeing.

Services Jordan Offers

Full Menu of Extension & Cut Services

Hand-Tied Extensions

Premium beaded rows for maximum blending and natural movement. Ideal for fine to medium hair textures seeking undetectable length and volume.

Tape-In Extensions

Lightweight, quick-application extensions perfect for maintaining a natural hairline. Comfortable to wear and easy to maintain between appointments.

Keratin Bond Extensions

Semi-permanent extensions fused directly to your hair using premium keratin. Long-lasting option with seamless integration and natural appearance.

Precision Haircuts

Expert cutting technique tailored to enhance your face shape and hair texture. Jordan creates silhouettes that complement both your extensions and overall look.

Blowouts

Professional blow-dry service to showcase your style between appointments. Perfect for special occasions or maintaining your everyday look.

Volume Consultations

Free consultation to discuss your hair goals, extension options, maintenance, and long-term care. Jordan helps you choose the best method for your lifestyle.
