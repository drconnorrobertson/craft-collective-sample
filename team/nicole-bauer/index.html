Nicole Bauer

Stylist & Texture Specialist

Nicole has a deep understanding of texture, curl patterns, and how different hair types respond to color and heat. With years of specialized training and hands-on experience, she excels at identifying the unique needs of each client and delivering results that enhance natural beauty rather than fighting against it.

Her expertise spans keratin smoothing treatments, custom blowouts, and textured haircuts designed to work with your hair's natural characteristics. Nicole's approach combines classic cutting techniques with modern styling methods, allowing her to create personalized looks that clients can maintain with confidence at home.

Whether you're looking to embrace your natural texture or explore new styling options, Nicole brings a collaborative approach to every appointment. She takes time to understand your lifestyle, maintenance preferences, and styling goals before creating a customized plan that works for you.

Specialties

Keratin Treatments Texture Styling Blowouts

Services Nicole Offers

Keratin Smoothing Treatments

Professional keratin treatments that reduce frizz, add shine, and make hair more manageable. Perfect for all hair types looking for smoothness without sacrificing volume and movement.

Custom Blowouts

Tailored blowout services designed for your hair type and styling preferences. Nicole creates voluminous, smooth, or textured finishes depending on your desired look and lifestyle.

Textured Haircuts

Expert cutting techniques for curly, wavy, and textured hair. Nicole understands how different cuts work with natural texture and creates styles that dry beautifully with minimal styling effort.

Curl Styling & Shaping

Specialized styling for natural and enhanced curls. From defining natural curl patterns to creating beautiful waves and curls, Nicole knows how to bring out the best in textured hair.

Color for Textured Hair

Color services specifically tailored for textured and curly hair types. Nicole understands how texture interacts with color and uses techniques that maintain hair health and vibrancy.

Deep Conditioning Treatments

Intensive conditioning treatments that restore moisture, repair damage, and improve overall hair health. Essential maintenance for colored, textured, and treated hair.
