Junior Stylist & Nail Artist

Rachel
Dominguez

Rachel is the newest member of the Craft Collective team, mentored directly by Derek Piekarski. She brings fresh energy and a sharp eye for detail to every service. What Rachel lacks in years, she makes up for in dedication, precision, and genuine care for each client's experience.

In addition to cuts and color, Rachel is a skilled nail artist offering gel, acrylics, and creative nail designs. Whether you're looking for a classic manicure or a bold statement design, Rachel approaches every nail service with the same level of artistry and attention to detail that defines Craft Collective. Her nail work seamlessly complements your hair transformation, completing the full salon experience.

Rachel's training under Derek ensures that every technique she applies meets Craft Collective's exacting standards. She is committed to ongoing education and staying current with the latest trends and methods. Booking with Rachel means working with someone who is both technically skilled and genuinely passionate about helping you feel your best.

Services

What Rachel offers

Women's Haircuts

Precision cuts tailored to your face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle. From subtle updates to transformative styles, Rachel's eye for proportion and movement ensures results that work with your natural hair.

Men's Haircuts

Clean lines, sharp fades, and classic styling. Rachel combines technical precision with a modern sensibility to deliver haircuts that look sharp and maintain their shape between visits.

Gel Nails

Long-lasting gel manicures with exceptional color selection and artistry. Rachel applies each coat with care to ensure durability and a flawless finish that lasts 3-4 weeks.

Acrylic Nails

Custom acrylic applications for length, strength, and style. Whether you prefer subtle elegance or bold statement nails, Rachel shapes and designs each set to enhance your natural beauty.

Creative Nail Art

From intricate designs to seasonal looks, Rachel brings artistic vision to your nails. Her designs complement your personal style while showcasing the level of craftsmanship Craft Collective is known for.

Basic Color Services

Foundational hair coloring including single-process color, toning, and basic highlights. Rachel's color consultation ensures your shade enhances your complexion and maintains hair health.
