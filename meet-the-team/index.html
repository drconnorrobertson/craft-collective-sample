Owner & Lead Stylist

Derek
Piekarski

Globally Recognized Hairdresser

Derek Piekarski is one of the most credentialed hairdressers in North America. He built his career training artists for seven of the industry's top hair brands, served on the North America Signature Artist Team for Wella Professionals, and led technical education for Aveda / Estee Lauder across an entire continent. He founded Craft Collective to bring world-class expertise to Pittsburgh.
