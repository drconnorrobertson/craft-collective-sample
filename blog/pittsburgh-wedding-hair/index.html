Wedding Hair

Pittsburgh Wedding Hair:
Why Brides Choose Us

March 5, 2026 · By Derek Piekarski · Craft Collective Salon Group

Your Wedding Day Hair Matters

Your wedding day is one of the most photographed days of your life. Every detail matters, and your hair sets the tone for your entire look. At Craft Collective Salon Group, we understand the pressure brides feel to get their wedding hair perfect. That is why we have built a wedding styling process designed to eliminate stress and deliver stunning results every time.

Why Pittsburgh Brides Trust Craft Collective

Pittsburgh has a vibrant wedding scene, from grand receptions at The Pennsylvanian to intimate ceremonies in the South Hills or vineyard weddings in Washington County. Wherever your venue is, our team has the experience to create a style that holds up through the ceremony, photos, dancing, and everything in between.

Our stylists are led by Derek Piekarski, who has styled hair for editorial shoots, celebrity events, and fashion shows around the world. That high-pressure experience translates directly to wedding day confidence. When you sit in our chair on your wedding morning, you are in the hands of a team that performs under pressure for a living.

Our Bridal Styling Process

Step 1: The Consultation

We start with a conversation about your vision. Bring reference photos, information about your dress and veil, and any ideas you have been collecting. We will discuss what works for your hair type, face shape, and the formality of your event.

Step 2: The Trial Run

Every bridal client at Craft Collective gets a trial appointment. This is a full run-through of your wedding day style, done weeks before the big day. You will leave the trial knowing exactly what your hair will look like, and we will document the style so we can recreate it perfectly on the day.

Step 3: Wedding Day Styling

On your wedding day, your stylist arrives prepared with everything needed to recreate your trial look. We build in time for adjustments, touch-ups, and any last-minute changes. Many of our bridal clients also book styling for their bridesmaids, mothers, and other members of the wedding party.

Popular Bridal Styles at Craft Collective

Our most requested bridal styles include classic updos, soft romantic waves, half-up half-down styles, braided details, and modern sleek looks. We also specialize in bridal color services. Many brides come to us months before their wedding to perfect their balayage or hair color so it photographs beautifully on the day.

Planning Your Wedding Hair Timeline

We recommend contacting us 6 to 12 months before your wedding date to secure your booking. Trial appointments are typically scheduled 4 to 8 weeks before the wedding. If you want to do a color transformation before the wedding, plan for that process to begin 3 to 6 months in advance, especially for significant changes like going blonde.

Venues We Serve Across Pittsburgh

We have styled brides getting married at venues across the greater Pittsburgh area, including the North Hills, Shadyside, Lawrenceville, South Side, Mt. Lebanon, Cranberry Township, Canonsburg, Washington County, and beyond. Whether you need on-site styling at your venue or prefer to visit our salon on the morning of your wedding, we can accommodate both.
