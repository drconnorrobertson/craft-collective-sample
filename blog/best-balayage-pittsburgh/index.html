Why Pittsburgh Clients Choose Craft Collective for Balayage

If you have searched for the best balayage in Pittsburgh, you have probably seen dozens of salons claiming to specialize in this technique. But balayage is not just a service you add to a menu. It is a freehand painting method that demands years of training, an eye for natural color movement, and deep knowledge of hair chemistry. At Craft Collective Salon Group, balayage is not something we offer on the side. It is one of our core specialties.

Our owner, Derek Piekarski, spent over a decade training artists for Wella Professionals and Aveda before opening Craft Collective. He served on the North America Signature Artist Team for Wella and received the Franz Stroher Global Education Master Trainer Award in 2016. That level of expertise is built into every balayage appointment at our salon.

What Is Balayage and Why Does Technique Matter?

Balayage is a French word meaning "to sweep." Unlike traditional foil highlights, balayage involves painting color directly onto the hair in sweeping motions. The result is softer, more natural-looking dimension that mimics the way the sun would lighten your hair. It grows out gracefully, meaning fewer touch-up appointments and lower long-term maintenance costs.

The quality of your balayage depends entirely on the skill of the colorist. Poor technique leads to harsh lines, uneven placement, or brassy tones. Our team of colorists has been trained by Derek personally, using the same techniques he taught to salon professionals across North America, Europe, and Asia.

Our Balayage Process at Craft Collective

Every balayage service at Craft Collective begins with a thorough consultation. We assess your natural base color, hair density, texture, and lifestyle. A client in Cranberry Township who spends time outdoors may want a different result than someone in Shadyside who works in an office. We tailor every application to your unique hair and your life.

We use Wella Professionals color exclusively for our balayage services. Derek's relationship with the brand means we have access to the latest formulations and techniques before most salons in the Pittsburgh area. Our custom toning process ensures your balayage never looks brassy or one-dimensional.

Balayage for Every Hair Type in Pittsburgh

Whether you have fine hair, thick hair, curly texture, or straight strands, balayage can work for you. We regularly see clients from across greater Pittsburgh, including North Hills, McCandless, Wexford, South Hills, Mt. Lebanon, Lawrenceville, and Squirrel Hill. Each one leaves with a result that is completely customized to their hair type and personal style.

If you have dark hair and want a subtle caramel balayage, we can do that. If you want a dramatic blonde transformation, we can build that over a series of appointments while keeping your hair healthy. We never rush the process or compromise hair integrity for speed.
