Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our hair salon services, booking, pricing, and locations in Pittsburgh and Canonsburg.

How do I book an appointment at Craft Collective Salon Group?
You can book online at any time through our Phorest booking page for the North Hills location at 2014D Babcock Blvd, Pittsburgh, PA 15209. For Canonsburg appointments at 115 W Pike St, Canonsburg, PA 15317, please call us directly at 724-514-7231. Online booking is available 24/7 and lets you select your stylist, service, and preferred time.
What hair services does Craft Collective offer?
Craft Collective Salon Group offers a comprehensive menu of hair services including balayage, highlights, blonding, full color, root touch-ups, glossing, toning, corrective color, hair extensions (hand-tied, tape-in, and keratin bond), keratin smoothing treatments, precision haircuts for women and men, lived-in color, and special occasion and wedding styling. We also offer nail services, skin care, facials, and lash extensions.
Where is Craft Collective Salon Group located?
We have two locations. Our primary location is at 2014D Babcock Blvd, Pittsburgh, PA 15209 in the North Hills, serving clients from McCandless, Ross Township, Cranberry Township, Wexford, and surrounding areas. Our second location is at 115 W Pike St, Canonsburg, PA 15317, serving the South Hills and Washington County. Call 724-514-7231 for both locations.
Who is Derek Piekarski?
Derek Piekarski is the owner and lead stylist of Craft Collective Salon Group. He is a globally recognized hairdresser who served on the North America Signature Artist Team for Wella Professionals and held the position of North America Manager of Technical Capabilities for Aveda and Estee Lauder. In 2016, he received the Franz Stroher Global Education Master Trainer Award, recognizing him as one of the top hair educators in the world. He has been featured in Vogue India and has served celebrity clients across the US, Europe, South America, India, and Canada.
How much does balayage cost at Craft Collective?
Balayage pricing at Craft Collective varies based on your hair length, density, current color, and the level of color change desired. We recommend booking a consultation so we can assess your hair and provide an accurate quote. Consultations allow us to plan the best approach and set realistic expectations for your balayage result.
Do I need a consultation before getting hair extensions?
Yes. A consultation is required before any extension service at Craft Collective. During the consultation, our extension specialist will assess your natural hair health, density, and scalp condition. We will discuss your goals, recommend the best extension method for your hair type, and provide accurate pricing based on the amount and length of hair needed.
What color brands does Craft Collective use?
We primarily use Wella Professionals color products. Our owner Derek Piekarski served on the North America Signature Artist Team for Wella and brings deep product expertise to every color service. We select our color lines based on performance, consistency, and hair health outcomes.
Does Craft Collective offer corrective color services?
Yes. Corrective color is one of our specialties. If you have experienced a bad color job at another salon, box dye damage, brassiness, uneven color, or over-processing, our team can create a plan to restore your hair to its best condition. Corrective color requires an in-person consultation because every case is unique.
What areas does Craft Collective serve?
Our North Hills location serves clients from across greater Pittsburgh including the North Hills, Shadyside, Lawrenceville, Squirrel Hill, South Hills, Cranberry Township, Wexford, Ross Township, McCandless, Mt. Lebanon, and beyond. Our Canonsburg location serves the South Hills and Washington County area. Clients regularly drive from across the region because of our team's expertise.
Does Craft Collective offer wedding and bridal hair services?
Yes. We offer comprehensive bridal styling including consultations, trial appointments, and wedding day styling for brides, bridesmaids, mothers, and wedding party members. We recommend booking 6 to 12 months before your wedding date. Trial appointments are typically scheduled 4 to 8 weeks before the wedding.
How often should I get my balayage touched up?
Most balayage clients visit every 12 to 16 weeks, which is significantly less frequent than traditional highlight maintenance. Because balayage creates a soft, graduated effect without a harsh root line, the grow-out looks natural and intentional. Your stylist will recommend a maintenance schedule based on your specific color and goals.
Does Craft Collective offer keratin treatments?
Yes. We offer keratin smoothing treatments that reduce frizz, improve manageability, and add long-lasting shine. These treatments are ideal for clients who want softer texture, faster blow-drying time, and smoother hair, especially during humid Pittsburgh summers. Results typically last 3 to 5 months depending on hair type and aftercare.
What should I do to maintain my hair color between appointments?
Use sulfate-free shampoo, deep condition weekly, use heat protection before styling, and wash your hair less frequently to preserve your color. UV-protecting products help prevent fading from sun exposure. Avoid hot water when washing your hair and rinse with cool water to seal the cuticle. Visit our hair care tips page for more detailed advice.
Is Craft Collective accepting new clients?
Yes. We are currently accepting new clients at both our North Hills Pittsburgh location on Babcock Blvd and our Canonsburg location on W Pike St. Book online or call 724-514-7231 to schedule your first appointment or consultation.
Does Craft Collective offer services beyond hair?
Yes. Craft Collective Salon Group offers a full menu beyond hair including nail services such as manicures, pedicures, gel, acrylics, and nail extensions, as well as skin care, facials, and lash extensions. We are a full-service salon destination.
What are Craft Collective's hours of operation?
Both locations are open Tuesday through Friday from 9:00 AM to 7:00 PM and Saturday from 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM. We are closed on Sunday and Monday. We recommend booking in advance, especially for weekend appointments, as they fill up quickly.
Can Craft Collective fix box dye damage?
Yes. Box dyes deposit metallic salts and other compounds that can react unpredictably with professional color products. Our team has extensive experience removing and correcting box dye buildup using specialized techniques. A consultation is required so we can assess the condition of your hair and create a safe correction plan.

Our entire team receives advanced training using the same techniques Derek has taught to salon professionals around the world.
