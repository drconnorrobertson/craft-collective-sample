World-class expertise,
Pittsburgh proud.

Derek Piekarski is not a stylist who stayed local and got good. He went out into the world, competed at the highest levels of the global hair industry, won its most prestigious awards, trained thousands of artists, and then chose to bring all of it back to Pittsburgh.

That decision -- to build something here instead of in New York or Los Angeles -- is what Craft Collective Salon Group is built on. Derek's philosophy is that every guest deserves the same level of thoughtfulness, technique, and personalization that he brought to training educators across an entire continent.

Today, Derek leads a team of stylists who share his commitment to ongoing education, advanced technique, and guest experience. Every service at Craft Collective reflects the standards he built his career on.
