Pittsburgh's home for elevated hair.

Craft Collective Salon Group was founded with a single conviction: that Pittsburgh deserves a salon built on world-class education, not just local talent. Owner Derek Piekarski spent decades training artists for seven of the industry's top hair brands before bringing that expertise home.

What sets Craft Collective apart is not just the credentials behind it -- it is the experience inside it. Every guest receives a personalized consultation, every service is executed with advanced technique, and every visit is designed around your hair's long-term health, not just the result in the chair.

We operate two locations -- North Hills Pittsburgh and Canonsburg -- serving clients from across greater Pittsburgh including Shadyside, Lawrenceville, Squirrel Hill, South Hills, Cranberry Township, Wexford, McCandless, and Mt. Lebanon. Our full-service menu spans hair, nails, skin care, and lashes.

We are accepting new clients. Book online or call 724-514-7231.

Our Philosophy

Three pillars

01

Style That Reflects You

Your hair is your personal statement. Whether you want a precision cut, a full color transformation, or the latest in texture trends, our team listens, consults, and executes to your vision -- not a template.

02

Education & Expertise

Our stylists train continuously, staying ahead of the latest techniques in color, balayage, extensions, and more. You receive not just a service, but a professional experience rooted in real expertise and ongoing education.

03

Long-Term Hair Health

We believe beauty and responsibility go hand-in-hand. Every service recommendation considers your hair's long-term health, not just today's result. We use thoughtfully selected color lines and technique-driven applications that protect your hair over time.
