Common Questions

Service FAQs

How long does a balayage appointment take?
A balayage appointment at Craft Collective typically takes 2.5 to 4 hours depending on your hair length, density, and the level of color change desired. A consultation helps us give you an accurate time estimate for your specific service.
What is the difference between balayage and highlights?
Balayage is a freehand painting technique that creates soft, blended, natural-looking dimension with no harsh lines. Traditional highlights use foils to saturate sections of hair from root to tip, creating more uniform brightness and lift. Balayage tends to grow out more naturally and requires less maintenance than foil highlights.
How much do hair extensions cost?
Extension pricing varies by method, hair length, and the amount of hair needed. A consultation is required before any extension service to assess your hair, discuss options, and provide accurate pricing. Call 724-514-7231 or book online to schedule your consultation.
How long do keratin treatments last?
Keratin smoothing treatments at Craft Collective typically last 3 to 5 months depending on your hair type, home care routine, and how frequently you wash your hair. Using sulfate-free shampoo extends results significantly.
Do you offer men's hair services?
Yes. We offer precision cuts, clean edging, beard shaping, and easy-to-maintain styles for men who want a sharp look with simple home upkeep.
